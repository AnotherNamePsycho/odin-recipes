Kimbap

Description

Kimbap is one of the most famous foods from the South Korean.

It a cooked rice that has a filling and rolled in dried seaweed.People usually confuse it with the japaneses sushi but it has more filling with more diverse ingredients and lack of vinegar taste.

Ingredients

  • Dried seaweed paper
  • Cooked rice
  • Beef
  • Egg
  • Yellow pickled radish
  • Carrot
  • Spinach
  • Burdock root
  • Sesame oil

How to make a kimbap

  1. Prepare the rice: Mix warm rice with a little sesame oil and salt.
  2. Prepare the filling: Season and cook each filling individually
  3. Assembly the kimbap: an even layer of rice on the rough side of the seaweed, leaving a 2-inch empty border at the top edge.Line up all your prepared fillings horizontally across the center of the rice.Use a bamboo mat to fold the bottom edge over the fillings and roll tightly. Use a dab of water on the top empty border to seal it shut.
  4. Finishing touch: Brush the outside of the finished roll with sesame oil for shine and flavor. Cut into bite-sized slices using a sharp, damp knife
  5. Ready to serve
