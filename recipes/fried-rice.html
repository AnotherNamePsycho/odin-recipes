Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is maybe the most easy and delicious dish you can do.

It come down to just throw a rice a vegetable and some meats in the pan and just stir it untill it cooked.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Egg
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • Diced onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil

How to make a Fried Rice

  1. Cook Veggies: Preheat a large skillet or wok to medium heat. Add sesame oil, chopped white onion, and frozen peas and carrots, then cook until tender.
  2. Eggs: Slide the vegetables to the side and then pour the eggs onto the other side of the skillet. Using a spatula, scramble the eggs. Once cooked, then you can mix the eggs with the vegetables.
  3. Add Rice: Add cooked white rice to the veggie and egg mixture, then pour soy sauce on top. Stir the rice and veggie mixture until warmed through and combined.
  4. Ready to serve
