Hawaiian Pizza

Description

Hawaiian Pizza was first made in italy. It is created because someone want to eat pizza with pine apple on it.

The taste is kinda sweet and sour. Which make alot of people don't like it.The Hawaiian Pizza usually contains pineapple cheese tomato and sometime bacon.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ham or bacon
  • Pineapple

How to make a Hawaiian Pizza

  1. Prep the Oven and Dough: Preheat oven to 425–475°F (220–245°C). On a floured surface, roll out the dough to a 12-inch circle and transfer to a greased baking sheet or pizza pan.
  2. Add Sauce and Cheese: Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce, leaving about a 1/2-inch border for the crust. Cover with a generous layer of mozzarella cheese.
  3. Add Toppings: Distribute the ham and pineapple evenly over the cheese.
  4. Bake: Bake on the lower rack for 10–15 minutes, until the crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbling.
  5. Readfy to serve
